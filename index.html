Our Mission

Welcome to FIZIKA! Our team's mission is to make olympiad education free, accessible, and effective for all students in Singapore, regardless of background. We aim to create a vibrant community of physics lovers to discuss all things physics, share insights, and foster a passion for the subject.

Lesson Syllabus & Schedule

Our curriculum comprehensively covers the Singapore Physics Olympiad (SPhO) syllabus. While we plan to offer multiple classes eventually, we are launching with our flagship SPhO training.

SPhO Training Class

Schedule: Saturdays, 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

This class will systematically go through the topics listed below. Detailed weekly schedules will be provided to registered participants.

Mechanics

  • Kinematics
  • Statics
  • Dynamics
  • Oscillations
  • Rotation
  • Gravitation
  • Fluid Mechanics

Electromagnetism (EM)

  • Electrostatics
  • Electric Current, DC Circuits
  • Magnetostatics
  • Lorentz Force
  • Electromagnetic Induction (EMI)
  • AC/RLC Circuits

Thermodynamics

  • Ideal Gases, Thermodynamics Laws
  • Methods of Heat Transfer
  • Statistical Mechanics

Waves & Optics

  • Wave Equation & Standing Waves
  • Interference & Diffraction
  • Geometrical Optics

Relativity

  • Kinematics
  • Dynamics
