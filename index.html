Our Mission

Welcome to our initiative! Our team's mission is to make physics olympiad education free, accessible, and effective for all students in Singapore. We aim to create a vibrant community of physics lovers to discuss all things physics, share insights, and foster a passion for the subject.

Lesson Syllabus & Schedule

Our curriculum is designed to comprehensively cover the Singapore Physics Olympiad (SPhO) syllabus. Specific schedules and time slots will be announced upon registration and participant availability.

Mechanics

  • Kinematics
  • Statics
  • Dynamics
  • Oscillations
  • Rotation
  • Gravitation
  • Fluid Mechanics

Electromagnetism (EM)

  • Electrostatics
  • Electric Current, DC Circuits
  • Magnetostatics
  • Lorentz Force
  • Electromagnetic Induction (EMI)
  • AC/RLC Circuits

Thermodynamics

  • Ideal Gases, Thermodynamics Laws
  • Methods of Heat Transfer
  • Statistical Mechanics

Waves & Optics

  • Wave Equation & Standing Waves
  • Interference & Diffraction
  • Geometrical Optics

Relativity

  • Kinematics
  • Dynamics

Schedule & Time Slots: Detailed schedules and specific time slots for lessons will be communicated to registered participants. We aim to offer flexible options to accommodate different school timetables.
